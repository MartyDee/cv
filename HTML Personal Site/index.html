Marty Dee Donovan

Co-founder of Medway Sound Ltd

Born in Leamington on January 30th 1967, Marty Dee Donovan was one of seven children of /Andrew and Helena Donovan . The family are originted from Ireland
with Andrew being born in Cork and Helena born in County Armargh in Northern Ireland. They Both met whilst Andrew was doing a stint in the Irish Military Police
and after his service time the were married and followed the family over to England.

The order of siblings went in this order;

  1. Andrew ( Father, deceased)
  2. Helena (Mother, deceased)
  3. Andrew (deceased)
  4. Alan
  5. Michael
  6. Helena
  7. Marty
  8. Kathy
  9. Caroline
    10.
Marty grew up in Lambeth, in Stockwell then on to Clapham and then when married in 1988 he moved to Brixton.
From Brixton he went to Catford after the break up of his marriage, then to St. Ives in Cambridgeshire, then down to Allhallows in Kent where he met and fell in love
with Katie and finally married on 30th September 2016 and now lives in Snodland in Kent.
